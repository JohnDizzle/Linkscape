# LinkScape Browser

**Fast access to your favorite websites, tabs, and imported browsing data.**

LinkScape Browser is a lightweight web browser for Windows designed to provide a simple, familiar, and efficient browsing experience. Easily manage tabs, favorites, browsing history, and imported browser data while enjoying quick access to the websites that matter most.

Whether you're browsing for work, research, or everyday use, LinkScape Browser helps keep your content organized and readily accessible.

## Features

### Multi-Tab Browsing
Open and manage multiple websites at the same time with a familiar tabbed interface.

### Favorites Management
Save your favorite websites and quickly access them whenever you need them.

### History & Most Visited
View recently visited websites and quickly return to pages you access most often.

### Browser Import Tools
Import favorites and browsing history from supported browsers to get started quickly.

### Search Provider Selection
Choose your preferred search provider for a personalized browsing experience.

### Custom Home Page
Set your own home page and tailor LinkScape Browser to your workflow.

### PDF Support
Open PDF files directly within the browser.

### Quick Access Tools
Use the built-in Command Center to access tabs, favorites, history, settings, and commonly used browser functions.
