# Privacy Policy for LinkScape Browser

**Developer:** John M. Doyle  
**Effective Date:** July 20, 2026

LinkScape Browser is designed to provide a simple and efficient browsing experience while respecting user privacy.

## Information Stored Locally

LinkScape Browser may store the following information on your device to provide browser functionality:

- Favorites and bookmarks
- Browsing history
- Most-visited page statistics
- Browser settings and preferences
- Home page configuration
- Imported favorites and history from supported browsers

This information is stored locally on your device and is used solely to support browser features.

## Data Collection

LinkScape Browser does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

The application itself does not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by the user through websites visited while using the browser.

## Web Content and Third-Party Services

When visiting websites, those websites may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Search providers selected by the user may also collect search and usage information in accordance with their respective privacy practices.

Users should review the privacy policies of websites and search providers they choose to use.

## Browser Import Features

When using the browser import feature, LinkScape Browser may access supported browser data stored on your device, including:

- Favorites or bookmarks
- Browsing history

Imported information remains on your device and is used only to populate LinkScape Browser's local data.

## Data Control

Users may:

- Delete browsing history
- Remove imported data
- Manage or delete favorites
- Change browser settings at any time

These actions can be performed through the application's user interface.

## Security

LinkScape Browser stores application data using standard Windows application storage mechanisms. While reasonable measures are taken to help protect locally stored information, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

LinkScape Browser is not specifically directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect application improvements or legal requirements. Updated versions will be made available with future releases.
